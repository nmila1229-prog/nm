Ambon

Ambon, the capital of Maluku Province, is known as “Ambon Manise” because of its natural beauty. This city offers various interesting tourist attractions, especially beaches with clear sea water and white sand such as Natsepa Beach and Liang Beach. Ambon is a suitable destination to enjoy natural beauty as well as culture.
